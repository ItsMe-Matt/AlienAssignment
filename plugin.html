Animate on Scroll Library

Animate on scroll library is a library/plugin that helps you animate elements as you scroll down and see them. This is incredibly useful when you are trying to add micro-interactions into your website. It helps your website look a little less like a clickable poster and really helps its style look less old fashioned and more modern.
